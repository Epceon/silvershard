Stellar Drift

Genre: Space survival & exploration
Platforms: PC (Steam)

Stellar Drift is a thrilling space survival and exploration game where you pilot your ship through dangerous asteroid fields, mysterious cosmic storms, and discover lost outposts. Upgrade your vessel, gather resources, and see how far you can drift among the stars!

Every journey in Stellar Drift is unique, with procedurally generated space environments and a variety of challenges.

Features:
– Dynamic space environments
– Deep upgrade system
– Atmospheric soundtrack
– Leaderboards and achievements
