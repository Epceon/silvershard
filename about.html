SilverShard Studios is a small indie game studio founded in Norway with a passion for crafting immersive, visually polished, and mechanically engaging experiences.

We’re currently a core team of two developers, combining backgrounds in design, code, art, and sound. We believe in gameplay-first development and use the power of tools like Unreal Engine and AI-assisted workflows to push the boundaries of what a small team can create.

Every game we build is rooted in personality, creativity, and the drive to deliver something memorable to our players. We don’t just make games — we build worlds worth visiting.
