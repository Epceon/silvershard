About SilverShard Studios

Silvershard Studios is an independent game development team based in Norway. Our goal is to create engaging and accessible games with a focus on strong visuals, enjoyable gameplay, and lasting replay value.

We are a small, dedicated group of two with a passion for sci-fi, action, and exploration games. Our projects are driven by feedback from our community, and we believe in continuous improvement through open testing and iteration.

Currently, we are working on several projects, including both PC and mobile titles. We value transparent communication and aim to keep our players updated throughout the development process.

Thank you for following our journey.
