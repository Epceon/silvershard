About SilverShard Studios

SilverShard Studios is an independent game development studio founded in Norway, dedicated to creating engaging and visually impressive experiences for players worldwide. Our team was brought together by a shared passion for sci-fi, action games, and the endless creative possibilities that game development offers. We believe in making games that challenge, inspire, and entertain – always with a strong focus on gameplay quality and player experience.

Since our founding, SilverShard Studios has aimed to stand out by combining strong artistic vision with modern, accessible game design. We draw inspiration from classic arcade titles, story-driven adventures, and everything in between, blending our influences into unique, replayable games for PC and beyond.

Our debut project, Void Vanguards, was born out of a desire to bring fast-paced action and sci-fi aesthetics together. As we continue to grow, we are excited to expand our portfolio with new adventures like Coco Island and Stellar Drift. Each game reflects our core values: creativity, player empowerment, and a relentless drive to raise the bar for indie gaming.

We are committed to listening to our players, responding to feedback, and building a welcoming community around our games. Whether you’re here for competitive action, relaxing exploration, or something in between, we hope you find a home in the worlds we create.

Thank you for supporting SilverShard Studios. The adventure has only just begun!
